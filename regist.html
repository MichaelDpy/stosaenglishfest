Stosa English Festival will feature five types of competition:

Speech

Participants will deliver prepared speeches on a given topic to a panel of judges.

Newcast

Participants will deliver a newscast-style presentation on a current event to a panel of judges.

Storytelling

Participants will deliver a selfmade story to be presented in front of the judges

General English Test

The participants will be tested in a general english test. The winner will be determined by the fastest and most precise in the work

Debate

Participants will engage in a debate on a given topic with another participant, with a panel of judges determining the winner.
